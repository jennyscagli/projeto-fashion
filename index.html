If you've ever tried to buy matching bridesmaid dresses for a wedding then you'll know
how difficult styling multiple women is. But Little Mix - the world's biggest current
girlband - have managed to get the whole dressing-the-same-but-not-the-same thing nailed.

‘But we've all got different styles and different bodies.
Jade will always be the one who's more tomboyish, whereas I'm quite
sexy and I like a leotard and thigh highs. Leigh is a fashionista.’
‘I'm a bit of both,' Perrie Edwards chips in, 'I like a bit of everything really.’

Perrie adds that, sometimes, the girls prefer dressing for a red carpet event to concert
styling - even if that means they’re in hair and make-up by 11am. ‘I think because the
majority of the time everything we wear is kind of stage performance looks that when we
get to the red carpet we’re like, “Oh my God, we get to be fun.” We just get to dress up, we
love fashion.’ Their favourite look, they agree, is the all pink ensemble they wore to the
Brit Awards earlier this year (2019), where they were awarded Best British Video for Woman Like Me.
